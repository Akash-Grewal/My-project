Welcome to Horse-Haven

Tucked away in the quiet countryside, Horse Haven is a place where life slows down, the air is fresh, and the bond between horse and rider is at the heart of everything we do. Whether you’re saddling up for the first time or you’ve been riding for years, you’ll find a sense of home here.

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

We offer down-to-earth, hands-on experiences that include riding lessons, horse boarding, training, youth camps, and peaceful trail rides through nature. Our barns are built with care, our pastures are wide open, and our horses are treated like family.

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Here at Horse Haven, we believe in:

  • Honest horsemanship
  • Hard work and heart
  • Building trust through time spent in the saddle
  • Creating a space where folks feel welcome, no matter their background

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

With open fields, sturdy fences, and the comforting sound of hooves on dirt, Horse Haven isn’t just a stable — it’s a way of life.
